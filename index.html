I’m Akanksha, an international student, pursuing my master’s here at UB. My program began in August of 2021.
I completed my undergraduate studies in Electronics and Communication Engineering in the year 2020. After my graduation, I briefly worked as a Network Optimization Engineer at Huawei Technologies, GSC India.
Throughout my undergraduate coursework, I became more interested in programming and mathematical subjects. Languages like C, C++, MATLAB, Embedded C and Python were something that I worked with throughout my Bachelor's and I enjoyed using them for my projects. During my junior year, I was introduced to Artificial Intelligence and have been absolutely drawn towards the field ever since. I co-authored a review paper on AI and while researching to write this paper, I literally entered a whole new world of AI. I knew then that I had to pursue Machine Learning as a career option.
I opted for the Electrical Engineering program at UB because of its flexibility in choosing inter-departmental subjects. I chose subjects in a way that enhanced my mathematical concepts pertaining to ML and honed my coding skills.
I’m a tech optimist and believe in the good that tech has to offer to our society. Despite all the negativity surrounding tech in general, I strongly believe that this narrative will drastically change in years to come.
Besides my academics, I’m an avid reader and enjoy reading fiction during my free time. I’m also passionate about astrophysics and dream of being a passenger in one of the space trips in the near future.
